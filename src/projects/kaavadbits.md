KaavadBits is a tabletop storytelling installation that embodies the narrator-patron perspective of *kaavad baanchana*, a Rajasthani storytelling tradition dating back roughly 400 years. Presented at ACM TEI'24 and awarded Special Mention at IndiaHCI'23.
